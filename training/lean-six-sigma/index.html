Lean Six Sigma Training & Certification

Learn how to improve processes, reduce waste, solve problems, and apply data-driven decision-making using Lean Six Sigma.

Program Focus

This training is designed for students, professionals, and organizations who want to build foundational capability in Lean Six Sigma, DMAIC, root cause analysis, quality improvement, and process improvement.

What You Will Learn

  • Lean Six Sigma principles
  • DMAIC: Define, Measure, Analyze, Improve, Control
  • Waste identification and process improvement
  • SIPOC and process mapping
  • Root cause analysis using 5 Whys and Fishbone Diagram
  • Pareto analysis and basic quality tools
  • Control planning and sustainment

Who Should Join?

  • Students preparing for internships, capstone, and employment
  • Professionals in operations, quality, analytics, and management
  • Organizations developing continuous improvement capability

Training Outcomes

  • Understand foundation-level Lean Six Sigma concepts
  • Apply DMAIC as a structured problem-solving framework
  • Use basic quality tools for process improvement
  • Prepare for Lean Six Sigma certification pathways
